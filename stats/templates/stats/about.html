How to Use

Once registered as a user, navigate to a Score Entry page, either 18 Holes, Front 9, or Back 9.
From here you can enter the date, course name and selected tees. You will have to enter the hole par which is automatically set to 4 for each hole.
For each hole enter your score, select whether or not you hit the fairway and GIR (green in regulation) and also putts.
